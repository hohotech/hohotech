Privacy Policy for Hoho

Effective Date: April 10, 2025

Hoho (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application, Hoho.

1. About the App

Hoho is designed primarily for parents of children aged 2 to 6 years who are interested in homeschooling support and early language learning. The app provides access to curated YouTube playlists in various languages to help kids learn through engaging video content.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users.

  • No user accounts are required.
  • No login or sign-up process exists.
  • We do not use cookies or track any personal data.
  • No data is collected from children or parents using the app.

3. How the App Works

When you select a language:

  • A short audio introduction plays.
  • The app then opens a publicly available YouTube playlist related to that language in a new browser tab.

All video content is hosted on YouTube and subject to YouTube’s Terms of Service.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses:

  • YouTube: to link to external playlists. We do not embed or collect data from YouTube. Any data collected by YouTube is governed by Google's privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy.

We do not use any advertising SDKs, analytics tools, or third-party trackers.

5. Children's Privacy

Although Hoho is intended for parents of young children, no personal information is collected from children using the app. We are fully committed to compliance with applicable privacy laws, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) where relevant.
